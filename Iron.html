Iron

The arrogant A Rank tank

Iron was an A Rank tank that left Sung Jin Woo to die, he was extremely arrogant and foolish but is now one of the strongest in Sung Jin Woo's shadow army. He uses an axe and shield to fight , he blocks with his shield but then strikes with an extremely strong powerful axe strike and decimate his opponents.
